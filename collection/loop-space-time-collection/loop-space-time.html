Loop SpaceTime-Quantum Series

The Loop SpaceTime – Quantum Series project is conceived as a progressive journey: from a toroidal space that folds back onto itself, through relational networks without hierarchy, to Quantum Graphity, where the reference to Loop Quantum Gravity becomes explicit.

In Loop Quantum Gravity, spacetime is discrete and relational, often represented as a network of nodes, and gravity emerges from the relations between them.
